Dinomania is a project devoted to teaching about dinosaurs. It's fascinating that the giant reptiles once walked the Earth. Feel free to explore and learn about all these amazing creatures!

Dinosaurs roamed the world for over 100 million years!

Mezozoic was the era in which the dinosaurs ruled the earth. It is divided into 3 periods.

Triassic lasted from 251 to 201 millions years ago. It was the time of the first dinosaurs.

Jurassic, 201 to 145 million years ago, was the time where all the continents formed one supercontinent: Pangea.

Cretaceous started 145 million years ago. It ended when 66 milion years ago, when the dinosaurs went extinct
